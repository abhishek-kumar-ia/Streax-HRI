Why HRI

At HRI, we've been building premium products with an equally exclusive team for over six decades. As a company dedicated to uncompromising quality, our people have always been our greatest support and top priority. With a strong belief in mutual growth, we provide our teams with equal opportunities to learn and excel, empowering them to make a difference in both the world of hair care and our communities.

Become a part of our story

We believe that performance speaks louder than promises. We walk the talk, and with each step, our team grows stronger, reaching new horizons and embarking on fresh innovations. Join us as we take this opportunity to elevate beauty standards while honouring our traditional ethics.

Mission

To empower individuals with self-expression with innovative and effective beauty and personal care products. Inspired by the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda and driven by the cutting-edge R&D, we are dedicated to set new industry standards that bring a change.

Vision

To empower individuals with self-expression with innovative and effective beauty and personal care products. Inspired by the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda and driven by the cutting-edge R&D, we are dedicated to set new industry standards that bring a change.

Life at HRI

Our people are at the heart of our success. In 2025, we proudly celebrated our sixth year as a certified Best Workplace to Work, an honour that reinforces the culture we’ve built together. It's a culture founded on credibility, respect, fairness, pride, and camaraderie. It's a place where your ideas are styled with our vision, your learning is paired with confident execution, and your passion meets our purpose..
